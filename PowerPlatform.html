Power Platform Products

The below are a selection of Power Apps I have created whilst at Springboard IT.

HOLIDAY REQUEST APP

An app for requesting, editing, viewing and accepting/rejecting holiday requests.

The app is in use at multiple companies, the largest having 70+ employees. Holiday balances are by the hour and the app caters for different shift patterns and rota days.

The app features login functionality (via a pin/password set by each employee). This helps differentiate between multiple users using one email address (for example workshop@) and helps catalogues users as either a Team Member or Team manager.

Members can request holiday and edit or view their holiday requests. In addition, Managers can accept/reject holiday requests and view holiday for their specific team (or if part of higher managements/HR) the whole company.

All holiday data is held in SharePoint, with accepted holiday requests appearing on a Calendar view which is also visible in the Company's Microsoft Teams (where the app is also embedded).

Power Automate

There are multiple flows in Power Automate:

  • On submit of a holiday request (and the request being patched to a SharePoint list) a flow ascertains who an individuals manager is and emails them details of the request; with accept/reject options.
    If accepted:
    • An email is sent to the requestee confirming their holiday has been approved, the request is updated to accepted in SharePoint and appears on the Calendar view.

TIMESHEET/LOCATION REGISTER APP

The app enables employees to clock in and out when arriving at different offices and acts as a site register; in the event of a fire etc.

Tablets at each of the company locations have set locations.

The app is use at one company but across a number of their sites.

A user simply enters their unique pin to clock in or out; if a user isn't currently clocked in they are done so upon input of their pin and vice versa if they are already clocked in at the point of entering their pin.
